Ghost Dungeon: A Ghost of a Chance

  • High Concept:
  • A puzzle game where the ghost must reach the treasure by using levers to move the impenetrable walls out of the way
  • Player Experience Goals:
  • Careful thinking and experimentation to find out the best way to open the way
  • Genre:
  • Puzzle
  • Game play:
  • You can move the ghost with left and right arrow keys. When you're close to a wheel, you can turn it by hitting SPACEBAR. If you find yourslef stuck, you can use the emergency reset wheel on the left. Once you reach the chest, open it with SPACEBAR to win! Play again by hitting ENTER
  • Prototype goal:
  • puzzle mechanic where one action causes several things to happen. Only the right order will win
  • Features:
  • The ability to reset the field at any time and careful thinking is needed to win.
  • Setting/Premise:
  • Set in a cave guarded by the Paddles. Exists in the Paddle/Ghost universe
  • Story:
  • It's been 10000 years since the Paddle Revolution ended and after enduring slavery for so long, the Ghosts have sent forth a champion, their spookiest ghost, to obtain a treasure that can help to save their people from the tyranny of the Paddles. But many obstacles lie in this ghost's path. Four of them. Walls.
  • Target Audience:
  • Ages 10-18
  • Play time:
  • Average of 1 minute
  • Strategies:
  • Test out which walls each wheel controls and determine the right order. Or trial and error
